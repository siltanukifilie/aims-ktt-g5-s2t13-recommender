# Dispatcher — Weekly Leads Workflow for Offline Artisans
## Made in Rwanda Content Recommender | G5 | S2.T1.3

---

## The Problem on the Ground

In Nyamirambo, Kigali, a leatherworker opens his workshop
every morning at 6am. He handcrafts leather boots, bags,
wallets and sandals. His products are high quality.
His prices are fair.

This week, 47 buyers searched online for exactly what he makes.
None of them found him.

They found Nike. They found Zara. They found Alibaba.

The artisan has:
- No smartphone
- No internet connection
- A basic Tecno feature phone that receives SMS
- Speaks Kinyarwanda and basic French
- Limited literacy

He is not the problem. The algorithm is the problem.
This system fixes the algorithm.

## Weekly Workflow — Step by Step

### MONDAY MORNING — System Runs Automatically
- Recommender processes all search queries from past 7 days
- Counts how many times each artisan category appeared in top 3
- Generates ranked leads report per artisan per district
- No human needed at this stage

### TUESDAY — Cooperative Agent Reviews
- Agent logs into leads dashboard
- Sees leather products appeared 47 times this week
- Nyamirambo leather category matched 23 queries
- Agent prepares weekly SMS in Kinyarwanda for each artisan

### WEDNESDAY 8AM — SMS Sent to Artisan

Artisan receives this on his basic Tecno phone:

### THURSDAY — Artisan Prepares Stock
- Reads the SMS
- If cannot read, neighbor or family member reads it
- Prepares the recommended stock quantities
- No internet needed
- No smartphone needed
- Just hands and workshop

### FRIDAY 9AM — Agent Visits Workshop
- Agent arrives at workshop in Nyamirambo
- Brings printed lead sheet with buyer query details
- Helps artisan photograph 3 products for marketplace
- Collects 2 products for cooperative showroom display
- Leaves receipt and next week schedule

### FRIDAY AFTERNOON — Sales Attempted
- Agent contacts buyers who searched matching queries
- Connects them directly with artisan products
- Cash payment collected on delivery
- No bank account needed for artisan

## Addressing Real Constraints

### No Smartphone
Basic Tecno feature phone is all that is needed.
SMS requires no data, no app, no internet.
Arrives like any other text message.
Works on 2G network available nationwide in Rwanda.

### Illiteracy
- Agent reads SMS aloud during Friday visit
- Voice call option available via 0788-300-100
- Neighbor or family member can read SMS

### Multiple Languages
- SMS delivered in Kinyarwanda
- Recommender understands English, French, Kinyarwanda
- Agent speaks Kinyarwanda with all artisans

### Intermittent Power
- SMS stored on SIM card without power
- No charging required to receive message
- Works offline completely

### No Bank Account
- Agent collects cash from buyer
- Delivers cash to artisan same day
- MTN Mobile Money option for artisans who have it

## The Core Principle

The system does not ask artisans to change.
It does not ask them to learn technology.
It does not ask them to get a smartphone.

It brings the market to them.
In their language.
On their device.
Through their trusted cooperative agent.

**The goal is not to digitize the artisan.
The goal is to digitize the opportunity.**
